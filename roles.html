Who We Are Looking For

We're currently building the founding team of a breakthrough interdisciplinary project focused on using artificial intelligence to identify antiviral candidates against the Foot-and-Mouth Disease virus (FMDV).

We're seeking researchers and specialists who are open to innovation, collaboration, and the possibility of contributing to a spin-off project. All listed roles are open for discussion. Let's build this together.

  • Expertise in virology, molecular biology, biochemistry or related field
  • Experience with research design and academic grant applications
  • Ability to co-author or supervise the scientific direction of the project
  • Willingness to engage with an interdisciplinary team (AI + chemistry)
  • Open to supporting a startup/spin-off model with partial IP protection
  • Ready to act as scientific mentor and lead scientific authority
  • Can help structure lab research phases
  • May co-sign or advise grant proposals
  • Experience with genome or protein analysis and sequence alignment
  • Ability to prepare and clean biological data for AI models
  • Collaborates closely with virologists and AI developers
  • Supports virtual screening and target validation
  • Familiar with viral structure and known mechanisms of action
  • Able to evaluate molecular candidates from a biological perspective
  • Experience with drug discovery, QSAR, molecular modeling or reinforcement learning
  • Builds or fine-tunes models for predicting molecular activity
  • Works with molecular representations (SMILES, graph-based models, etc.)
  • Optimizes compound libraries based on target profiles
  • Supports virtual screening and de novo molecular design
  • Coordinates with biology/chemistry to adjust model parameters
  • Designs potential antiviral molecules
  • Understands structure-activity relationships (SAR)
  • Able to evaluate chemical feasibility and toxicity
  • Prepares compound datasets and libraries
  • Supports translation from AI-generated designs to testable molecules
  • Collaborates with lab team to define experimental steps
  • Provides expert knowledge of the virus structure and replication
  • Advises on potential molecular targets within the viral life cycle
  • Helps define laboratory assays and biological testing pipeline
  • Coordinates with chemistry and bioinformatics
  • May contribute to writing scientific papers and grant proposals
  • Assists in assessing the biological effect of selected compounds
  • Ensures lab operations and material handling
  • Coordinates biological assays and test setups
  • Familiar with antiviral or BSL2+ or BSL3 lab environments
  • Keeps track of experimental results and documentation
  • Supports communication between lab and other team parts (AI, coordination)
  • Helps with sample preparation and safety compliance
  • Oversees team communication and progress tracking
  • Manages timelines, external relations, and administrative matters
  • Connects scientific and technological streams
  • Drives the vision and fundraising efforts
  • Coordinates outreach to universities and institutions
  • Assists in building a spin-off structure and legal framework
